Recipe #1

Roast Potatoes

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4 people
  • Secret: really really tasty
Ingredients Quantity
Maris Piper Potatoes 1 kg
duck or goose fat fat 100 g
flour 2 tsp

Step 1:Put a roasting tin in the oven
Step 2: Peel the 1kg potatoes and cut each into 4 even-sized pieces if they are medium size
Step 3: Drop the potatoes into a large pan and pour in enough water to barely cover them.

Recipe #2

Baked Cheesecake

  • Time: 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Serves: 12 people
  • Secret: best desert ever!
Ingredients Quantity
butter 85 g
Philadelphian cheese 900 g
golden caster sugar 250 g

Step 1: Heat the oven to 180C/ 160C fan/ gas 4.
Step 2: Melt 85g butter in a medium pan. Stir in 140g digestive biscuit crumbs and 1 tbsp golden caster
Step 3: Press the mixture into the bottom of the pan and bake for 10 minutes.
